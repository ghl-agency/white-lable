### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a long-term contract?**  
No. The platform operates on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts required. Accounts can be upgraded, downgraded, or canceled directly from the dashboard at any time.

**Does this platform replace other tools?**  
It functions as an all-in-one system that consolidates CRM, funnel building, email marketing, appointment scheduling, SMS capabilities, and review management functions typically handled by separate services.

**Is a free trial available?**  
Yes. A 14-day free trial provides full access to the selected plan features.

**Is technical expertise required?**  
The interface is designed for usability. Support resources, pre-built templates for multiple industries, and community assistance are available to facilitate setup.

**What is the primary difference between Starter and Unlimited plans?**  
The Starter plan supports up to 3 sub-accounts while the Unlimited plan provides unlimited sub-accounts along with additional reporting and rebilling options.

**What does SaaS Mode enable in the Agency Pro plan?**  
SaaS Mode allows automated creation of client sub-accounts and supports direct subscription sales from an agency’s own website.

**What does “unlimited” include?**  
All plans provide unlimited contacts and unlimited team users. Sub-account limits vary by plan tier.

**Can custom domains and branding be applied?**  
Yes. Custom domains can be connected for funnels and websites, and full white-label branding is available for the client interface.

**Are there additional fees?**  
Platform access is covered by the subscription. Usage-based fees apply for SMS, email, phone numbers, and premium AI features. Higher-tier plans support rebilling these costs to clients.

**Can plans be changed after signup?**  
Yes. Upgrades and downgrades are available with prorated billing.
