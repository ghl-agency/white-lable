# Billing and Pricing Management – March 2026

Agencies managing client subscriptions across separate billing platforms deal with missed charges, manual invoice creation, and payment failures that go unresolved. Each disconnected tool adds administrative overhead and increases the likelihood of revenue leakage. GoHighLevel White Label includes a native billing system that handles subscription management, pricing configuration, and automated payment collection inside the same platform agencies use to run client operations, accessible through the [white label billing and subscription platform](https://www.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=smetrix28).

---

## Subscription Management

The billing module allows agencies to create and manage client subscriptions directly from the master account. Pricing plans are configured once and assigned to client sub-accounts as documented in [Client Management](/white-lable/client-management/).

- Create unlimited pricing tiers with custom feature access per tier
- Assign clients to plans based on service level
- Upgrade or downgrade client subscriptions without manual intervention
- Set trial periods and automate conversion to paid plans
- Manage subscription cancellations and account suspensions

---

## Pricing Configuration

Agencies define their own pricing structure independent of what GoHighLevel charges at the agency level. The markup and packaging decisions belong entirely to the agency.

- Configure monthly, quarterly, or annual billing cycles
- Apply usage-based charges for SMS, email, and phone credits
- Bundle platform access with service retainers under a single invoice
- Create one-time setup fees separate from recurring subscription charges

This pricing flexibility supports the service packaging strategies that agencies implement across the client accounts managed through [CRM and Pipeline Management](/white-lable/crm-pipelines/).

---

Agencies ready to consolidate client billing inside their white label platform can access the full billing system [here](https://www.gohighlevel.com/?fp_ref=smetrix28).

## Automated Invoicing and Payment Collection

The system generates invoices automatically based on the billing cycle assigned to each client. Payment collection runs without manual initiation.

- Automate recurring invoice generation on billing cycle dates
- Process payments through connected payment gateways
- Send automated payment receipts to clients under the agency brand configured in [Branding and Customization](/white-lable/branding-customization/)
- Trigger dunning sequences for failed payments automatically
- Log all payment activity at the sub-account level

---

## Revenue Reporting

Payment and subscription data feeds into the performance reporting environment documented in [Reporting and Analytics](/white-lable/reporting-analytics/). Agencies track revenue by client, by plan tier, and by billing cycle without exporting data to a separate tool.

---

## Integration With Platform Operations

Billing operates alongside every other platform function. Client accounts provisioned through [Client Management](/white-lable/client-management/) are assigned billing plans as part of the same setup process. Automations configured in [Marketing Automation and Workflows](/white-lable/marketing-automation/) can trigger based on payment events such as successful charges or failed transactions.

Access to billing configuration is restricted by the role structure outlined in [Team and Permission Controls](/white-lable/team-permissions/). Third-party accounting or payment tools can be connected through [Integrations and API Capabilities](/white-lable/integrations/). Client-facing billing communications follow the same branding applied to [Appointment Scheduling System](/white-lable/appointment-scheduling/) confirmations, [Reputation Management Tools](/white-lable/reputation-management/) requests, and all other automated messages. The [Social Planner](/white-lable/social-planner/), [Funnel and Landing Page Builder](/white-lable/funnels-landing-pages/), and [AI Agents](/white-lable/ai-agents/) operate within the same unified account environment where billing is managed.
